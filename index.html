VOW OF THE DISCIPLE
deepsight weapon chest glpyhs
Room 1

Right after you drop down after this there will be a hidden room if you turn around. The crux is just to the left of the door.

Room 2

Straight ahead when entering the room with all the frozen things in blocks there is a room. The crux is right above the door.

Room 3

This is in the Acquisition room, over on the left past the sphinx statue is the next room. You will be directly above the “Grief” room. The crux is at the feet of the Sphinx statue.

Room 4

In the area named “Collection” the room is straight across from where you enter. The crux is up behind a statue on a ledge above the room.

Room 5

In the final stand area after finishing Caretaker, facing up the stairs, the room will be off to your right. The crux is to you left tucked in a corner in the shadows.

Room 6

After Caretaker, in the first room with enemies during the jump puzzle, the room is on the left side when you enter past a white statue. The crux is above the white statue.

Room 7

In the second room with enemies during the jumping puzzle, after the second secret chest, the room is on the right when you drop out of the ceiling. The crux is tucked to the right of the door in a corner next to some large gold cubes.

Room 8

Right after the relic encounter head toward the left stairs, the room is up above you on your left behind a statue. The crux is past the statues floating on the outer edge of the map.

Room 9

On the top floor with enemies, before jumping down to the platforms that extend out, the room is on the right side if facing the boss arena. The crux is high up in the shadows directly opposite the boss arena.
